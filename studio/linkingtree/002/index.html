Higashino Keigo

Keigo is a Japanese author born in Osaka, Japan in 1958. He is mainly known for his mystery novels. He In 2006, he won the 134th Naoki Prize for The Devotion of Suspect X.The English translation of The Devotion of Suspect X was nominated for the 2012 Edgar Award for Best Novel and the 2012 Barry Award for Best First Novel. He writes not only mystery novels but also essays and story books for children. The style of writing differs from his novels, but basically he does not use as many characters as in his novels.
